How we vet

Our vetting process

Only a small fraction of applicants make it onto the SparkleClean team. Here's the four-step process every cleaner completes.

1

Apply & ID check

Cleaners apply with proof of identity and their right to work, which we verify before moving them forward.

2

DBS & references

We run an enhanced DBS check and take up professional references to confirm a trustworthy track record.

3

In-person interview

Every candidate meets our team face to face so we can gauge their standards, reliability and attention to detail.

4

Ongoing quality checks

Once on board, cleaners are monitored through customer feedback and spot checks to keep standards high.
