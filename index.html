Good to know

Your questions, answered

Everything you might be wondering before you book.

Are your cleaners insured and vetted?

Absolutely. Every cleaner is fully insured and DBS-checked, with references verified before they ever set foot in your home. Your peace of mind comes first.

Do I need to provide cleaning products?

It's your choice. Many customers prefer we use their own products, but we're happy to bring professional, eco-friendly supplies at no extra cost — just let us know when you book.

What if I'm not home — how do keys work?

Most regular customers give their cleaner a key or key-safe code for convenience. Every key is logged, labelled anonymously and stored securely. You're always in control of access.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Of course. Life happens. You can reschedule or cancel any clean with at least 24 hours' notice, free of charge — no long contracts, no cancellation fees.

How do you vet your cleaners?

Every applicant goes through ID verification, a DBS check, reference checks and an in-person interview. Only around 1 in 5 make it onto the SparkleClean team.
