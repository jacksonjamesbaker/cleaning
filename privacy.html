Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data when you ask SparkleClean for a cleaning quote. This document is a template and should be reviewed by a qualified solicitor before going live.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

1. Introduction & who we are

SparkleClean Ltd ("SparkleClean", "we", "us" or "our") provides domestic and commercial cleaning services and matches customers with vetted local cleaners across the UK. We are the data controller responsible for the personal data described in this policy.

This policy explains what personal data we collect when you request a quote or otherwise interact with us, how we use it, and the rights you have under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. If you have any questions, contact us at hello@sparkleclean.co.uk.

2. What information we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data, most of which you provide directly through our quote form:

  • Identity data — your name and, where provided, your title.
  • Contact data — your email address, mobile telephone number and postcode.
  • Service requirements — details about the cleaning you need, such as service type, property size, frequency and any special instructions.
  • Marketing & attribution data — campaign parameters (for example UTM tags) that tell us how you found us, and your marketing preferences.
  • Technical data — your IP address, browser type and version, device information and general usage data collected automatically when you visit our website.

We do not intentionally collect special category data (such as health or ethnicity). Please do not include sensitive information in free-text fields.

3. How we collect it

  • Directly from you — when you complete our online quote form, contact us by email or telephone, or otherwise correspond with us.
  • Automatically — as you navigate our website, through cookies and similar technologies (see section 8).
  • From third parties — for example advertising and analytics platforms that help us understand how our campaigns perform.

4. How we use your data & our lawful basis

We only use your personal data where the law allows us to. The most common lawful bases we rely on are:

  • To provide quotes and deliver our services — to prepare an estimate, arrange bookings and manage our relationship with you. Lawful basis: performance of a contract, and our legitimate interests in responding to your enquiry.
  • To match you with a suitable cleaner — sharing the minimum information needed for a cleaner to attend. Lawful basis: legitimate interests.
  • To send marketing — where you have asked to hear from us. Lawful basis: your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.
  • To improve and secure our website — analytics, troubleshooting and fraud prevention. Lawful basis: legitimate interests.
  • To comply with legal obligations — such as accounting and tax requirements. Lawful basis: legal obligation.

5. Marketing & attribution

When you arrive from an advertisement or campaign link, we may store attribution parameters (such as UTM tags) for the duration of your browsing session so we can understand which campaigns lead to enquiries. This information is associated with your quote request if you choose to submit one.

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by emailing hello@sparkleclean.co.uk. Opting out of marketing will not stop service-related messages about a booking you have made.

6. Sharing your data

We share personal data only where necessary, and never sell it. Recipients may include:

  • Vetted cleaners we match you with, who receive the details required to prepare and attend your clean.
  • Service providers acting on our behalf, such as hosting, IT, email, analytics and payment providers, under appropriate contractual safeguards.
  • Professional advisers and authorities where we are required to do so by law or to protect our legal rights.

Where any provider processes data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards (such as UK approved standard contractual clauses) are in place.

7. Cookies & similar technologies

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies to make our website work, remember your preferences and measure how our marketing performs. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings or any cookie banner we display. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site functions.

8. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy. Quote enquiries that do not lead to a booking are typically retained for up to 24 months so we can follow up and understand demand, after which they are deleted or anonymised. Records relating to bookings and payments are kept for as long as required to meet our legal and accounting obligations.

9. Your rights under UK GDPR

Subject to certain conditions, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data where there is no good reason for us to keep it.
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on our legitimate interests, and to direct marketing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@sparkleclean.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK supervisory authority, at ico.org.uk — although we would appreciate the chance to resolve your concern first.

10. How we keep your data secure

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss or misuse, including access controls, encryption in transit and staff confidentiality obligations. No transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to protect your information and limit access to those who need it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or the law. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you.

12. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@sparkleclean.co.uk. Ready to book? You can get a quote at any time.
