Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know about our cleaners, products, pricing and guarantees — all in one place. Can't find your answer? We're only a message away.

Are you fully insured?

Yes. SparkleClean is fully insured, with comprehensive public liability cover as standard. In the unlikely event that something is damaged during a clean, you are protected. We are happy to share our insurance details on request before your first visit.

How are your cleaners vetted and DBS-checked?

Every cleaner is interviewed in person, reference-checked and DBS-checked before they ever set foot in a customer's home. We verify their right to work in the UK and only take on cleaners we would happily welcome into our own homes. Your safety and peace of mind come first.

Do I need to provide cleaning products and equipment?

It is entirely up to you. Many customers prefer us to use their own products so they know exactly what is going onto their surfaces, while others ask us to bring everything. We are happy to supply our own kit, including eco-friendly and fragrance-free options, at no extra cost. Just let us know your preference when you get a quote.

What if I'm not home? How do you handle keys and access?

Most of our customers are out at work while we clean, which is no problem at all. You can leave a key in a secure key safe, provide an entry code, or make whatever arrangement suits you. Any keys are securely logged, never labelled with your address, and handled with strict care. Of course, you are welcome to be at home too.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

Life happens, so rescheduling is easy. We simply ask for at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or move a booking so we can offer the slot to another customer and keep your cleaner's day fair. There are no long contracts and no cancellation fees when you give reasonable notice. Just get in touch and we'll sort it out.

What does "from £X per hour" actually mean?

Our advertised rate is the starting hourly price for regular domestic cleaning. Your exact quote depends on the size of your home, how often you'd like us to visit and any extras such as ironing or inside-oven cleaning. There are no hidden charges — you'll see a clear, all-in price before you book. Get a quote and we'll confirm your figure in writing.

What's included in a regular clean?

A regular clean covers the everyday essentials: dusting, vacuuming and mopping floors, cleaning and sanitising kitchens and bathrooms, wiping surfaces, emptying bins and tidying as we go. You can tailor the checklist to your priorities, and add extras like ironing, interior windows or inside-the-oven cleaning whenever you need them.

Is there a minimum number of hours?

Yes, we have a small minimum booking, typically two hours per visit, so your cleaner has enough time to do a thorough job you'll be delighted with. When you request a quote we'll recommend the right number of hours for your home so nothing feels rushed.

Will I have the same cleaner each visit?

Yes. We match you with a regular cleaner who gets to know your home and exactly how you like things done. Should your cleaner ever be off sick or on holiday, we'll arrange a trusted, fully vetted cover cleaner so your routine isn't disrupted.

What if I'm not happy with the clean?

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If anything isn't up to standard, let us know within 24 hours and we'll return to put it right free of charge under our re-clean guarantee. We'd always rather fix something than leave you anything less than delighted.

Which areas of the UK do you cover?

We provide cleaning services across the UK through our network of local, vetted cleaners. When you enter your postcode for a quote we'll confirm availability in your area straight away. If we're not yet in your neighbourhood, we'll let you know honestly rather than leave you waiting.

How do I pay?

Payment is simple and cashless. Most customers pay by secure Direct Debit or card after each clean, so there's no need to leave money in the house. You'll receive a clear receipt every time, and you can change or pause your plan whenever you like.

How quickly can I book a clean?

Often within a few days. Once you request a quote we'll get back to you within one working hour, match you with a suitable local cleaner and agree a start date that works for you. For one-off deep cleans and end-of-tenancy jobs we can frequently fit you in at short notice.

Do I need to be home during the clean?

Not at all. You're welcome to be home if you'd prefer, but most customers give us secure access and come back to a spotless home. Whatever suits your routine, your cleaner will always treat your home with the utmost care and respect.

Still have questions?

Get a quote and our friendly team will happily answer anything else you'd like to know — no pressure, no obligation.
